Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Video Automation ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, protects, and discloses your information when you use our automation workflow service ("Service"). Our Service is built to facilitate the automated publishing of content to social media platforms, including TikTok, via official API integrations.

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application to connect with your TikTok account (or other supported platforms), we only request the minimum permissions (scopes) strictly necessary to provide the automation functionality. The data we collect includes:

  • Authentication Data: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by TikTok to authenticate our application's requests on your behalf.
  • Basic Profile Information: Account ID, username, display name, and profile picture URL solely to verify the correct account is connected and to display the connection status within our internal interface.
  • Content Data: Video files, titles, descriptions, and metadata that you explicitly input into our workflow to be published to your TikTok account.
  • Technical and Log Data: Error logs, API response codes, and timestamps generated during the execution of automation tasks (used exclusively for debugging and ensuring system reliability).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity with TikTok and establish a secure API connection.
  • To execute the core functionality of the Service: uploading and publishing your specified video content to your TikTok account.
  • To monitor the performance of our automation workflows and troubleshoot technical errors.
  • To comply with our legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We explicitly declare that we DO NOT use your data to train artificial intelligence (AI) models, nor do we use it for targeted advertising.

3. Data Retention and Storage

Video Automation operates primarily as a transient pass-through automation tool.

  • Transient Processing: Video files and metadata processed through our workflows are kept in temporary memory only for the duration required to successfully transmit them to TikTok's servers. We do not maintain a permanent media database of your videos.
  • Token Storage: OAuth tokens are stored securely using industry-standard encryption within our automation environment to maintain the active connection.
  • Log Retention: System execution logs (which may contain basic account IDs but no passwords or content) are retained for a maximum of 30 days for debugging purposes before being automatically purged.

4. Third-Party Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information or TikTok data to any third parties. We only share information under the following limited circumstances:

  • With the Platforms Themselves: Data is inherently transmitted to TikTok's API to fulfill your publishing requests. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, or investigate fraud.

5. Data Deletion and User Rights

You retain full ownership and control over your data. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.

  • Revoking Access: You can instantly revoke our application's access to your account by visiting your TikTok App settings (Settings and Privacy > Security and Login > Manage app permissions) and removing "Video Automation".
  • Complete Data Deletion Request: To request the complete removal of your authentication tokens, profile data, and any associated logs from our systems, please send an email to jessicajung01012000@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We are committed to processing and fulfilling your deletion request within 7 business days and will send you a confirmation email once completed.

6. Security Measures

We implement robust, industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transit and at rest. All communications between our Service and third-party APIs (including TikTok) are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols. Access to the underlying automation infrastructure is strictly limited and protected by multi-factor authentication.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for use by adults and businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take immediate steps to delete it from our records.
