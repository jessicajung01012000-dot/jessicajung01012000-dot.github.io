User Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: n8n-auto-video | Business: Jessicajung

Compliance with Meta Platform Developer Policies

1. Data Storage Statement

The n8n-auto-video application is a private internal tool used exclusively for publishing content to our own Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts. We do not save, record, or maintain any databases containing personal information of public users who interact with our pages.

2. Your Right to Data Deletion

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Meta's Developer Policies, users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data. Although our system does not hold such data, we provide a direct channel for you to submit a formal deletion request to ensure full compliance and peace of mind.

3. How to Submit a Data Deletion Request

If you wish to formally request the verification and deletion of any potential data related to your Facebook or Instagram profile, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at: pmpprofessor@outlook.com
  2. Use the exact subject line: Data Deletion Request - n8n-auto-video
  3. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your full name.
    • The URL to your Facebook or Instagram profile.
    • A brief statement requesting the deletion of your data.

4. Processing Time

Upon receiving your request, our technical team will manually query our internal n8n logs to verify that no data associated with your profile URL exists. We will process your request and send you a confirmation email of the deletion (or confirmation that no data was found) within 3 to 5 business days.
