Privacy Policy

App Name: n8n-auto-video | Business: Jessicajung

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for the n8n-auto-video application, operated by Jessicajung. This application is designed exclusively as an internal workflow automation tool to manage and publish content to our owned and operated Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts.

2. Information We Collect

Our application operates strictly on a server-to-server basis using Meta Graph API. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from public Facebook or Instagram users. The application only utilizes system-level access tokens required to publish content on behalf of the Page administrator.

3. How We Use Information

The system access tokens and Page IDs are used solely for the purpose of programmatic content posting (e.g., uploading videos, creating posts). We do not use this application to harvest user comments, messages, profiles, or any engagement metrics that contain Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because no public user data is collected, no user data is shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third-party services, advertisers, or external entities.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures within our internal infrastructure (n8n) to protect our administrative access tokens and prevent unauthorized access to our social media assets.
